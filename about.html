About mufascore.com

Created by Benjamin Barlow, in the spring of 2025. Benjamin has been playing in MUFA for almost a decade and decided to finally tackle a scoreboard that is specifically designed for coed ultimate frisbee. And this is it!

The code that runs this site is open source. Feel free to fork/contribute/comment/etc at github.com/bflbarlow/mufascore.com.
